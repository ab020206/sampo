Computer Engineering

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Math-I

    Calculus and Differential Equations.

  • Engineering Physics

    Optics and Quantum Mechanics.

  • Basic Electrical

    Circuit theory and AC/DC systems.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures

    Arrays, Lists, Trees, and Graphs.

  • Discrete Math

    Logic, Set Theory, and Combinatorics.

  • Digital Electronics

    Logic Gates and Circuit Design.

Third Year (TE)

  • Database Management

    SQL, NoSQL, and Schema Design.

  • Theory of Computation

    Automata and Formal Languages.

  • Computer Networks

    OSI Model and TCP/IP Protocols.

Fourth Year (BE)

  • Machine Learning

    Supervised and Unsupervised learning.

  • Information Security

    Cryptography and Cyber Security.

  • Project Phase-II

    Final implementation and thesis.
